    • Need A Firearm Shipped To You?

    Armory Up provides local FFL transfers for guns shipped from other dealers. Please call ahead or stop in to arrange the transfer. Our standard transfer fee is $25.00 and $15.00 for military/veterans, law enforcement and first responders. We will need your dealers contact information, including their phone number and email as well as your name and order number if you have one. After we receive a copy of the dealer's license we will have the gun shipped to our store and notify you when it is available for pick up.
    Note: we do not provide transfers for class 3 products. Add $5.00 for each additional firearm being transferred. For multiple handgun and rifle transfers each firearm transferred will be full cost.
